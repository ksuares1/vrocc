EL DÍA DE LOS MUERTOS

Veronica Robles Cultural Center (VROCC) to host The 7th Annual “Día de los muertos”, this year celebrating the lives of COVID-19 victims and honoring Pandemic first responders. Activities will take place on November 1st. From 11 AM to 8 PM.

The Dancing Elotes

VROCC's Dancing Elotes project provides jobs to youth and brings art, culinary traditions and traditional dance to the East Boston Farmers Market every Wednesday. This project allows VROCC to reach a broad spectrum of people, support artists and partner with other community organizations.

Live Perfomances

Connecting and engaging with our community has been so important during the pandemic. We have decided to move some our classes online, so that we can stay connected and keep learning about our culture and heritage going.
