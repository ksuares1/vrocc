VROCC Classes & Performances



Dance & Virtual Classes

VROCC offers a variety of dance and virtual classes to honor and celebrate our Latin culture.

Heritage Through Movement

At VROCC, our mission is to celebrate, encourage, and promote awareness of Latin culture. One of the ways in which we are able to accomplish this goal is through teaching community members about about our historical dances, food, and music. Throughout the week we offer a variety of classes to get our members engaged through movement and the lyrical sounds of our ancestors.

Monday

Danzas folklóricas Latinoamericanas Incluye danzas de Colombia, México, Perú, El Salvador, Guatemala, entre otros países.

Latin American Folkloric Dance Classes Traditional dance from several countries of Latin America including Colombia, México, Perú, El Salvador, Guatemala, among others.

Tuesday

Danza Afro-Brasileña La capoeira es una disciplina que combina artes marciales, danza, música y canto.

Afro Brazilian Dance Capoeira is a discipline that combines martial arts, dance, music and singing.

Wednesday

Danzas Chilenas Incluye la tradicional Cueca Chilena y danzas de otras regiones de Chile.

Chilean Dance Includes the traditional Cueca and other styles from several regions of Chile.

Thursday

Grupo de Mariachi Incluye los siguientes instrumentos, violín, guitarra, guitarrón, vihuela, trompeta y voz. Teoria de la música.

Mariachi Ensemble Includes violin, guitar, guitarron (mariachi bass), vihuela (5 string mariachi instrument), and trumpets. Music theory.
